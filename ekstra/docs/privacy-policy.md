# EKSTRA Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30

EKSTRA is a local-first overtime, shift, salary and extra income tracking app.

## Data Stored On Device

The app stores overtime entries, shift settings, salary settings, notification preferences, local account profile information, local entitlement state and backup data on your device.

## Data Sharing

EKSTRA does not operate its own server and does not upload your local tracking data to an EKSTRA backend.

If account login is enabled, Supabase may process your email address and authentication data so that you can sign in. If ads or in-app purchases are enabled in a published build, Google Play services, Google Mobile Ads, Google Play Billing or store platform services may process data according to their own policies.

## Backups

The app can export and import JSON backups when you choose to use those features. You control where exported backup data is stored or shared.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, EKSTRA uses local notifications on your device for shift, overtime, salary day and monthly summary reminders.
